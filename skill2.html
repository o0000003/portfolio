Co Mam.corp branding project .

"Co Mam" is a vibrant and authentic Vietnamese restaurant bringing the rich flavors and cultural essence of Vietnam to the heart of Hangzhou, China. The branding project aims to capture the essence of Vietnamese cuisine and hospitality while reflecting the modern and inviting atmosphere of the restaurant.

Packaging Design: Co Mam's packaging design reflects the restaurant's commitment to sustainability and quality. Takeaway containers and bags are made from eco-friendly materials, adorned with signature plastic stool and vibrant color element inspired by Vietnam in the 1970s. Each package is designed to enhance the dining experience and leave a lasting impression on customers.

Menu Design: The menu design for Co Mam is a feast for the eyes, featuring an array of delectable Vietnamese dishes presented with enticing descriptions. The menu is organized into categories such as appetizers, soups, noodles, and drink, making it easy for customers to navigate and explore the diverse offerings. The design is sleek, modern, and easy to read, reflecting the restaurant's commitment to excellence in both cuisine and presentation.

Poster Design: The poster design for Co Mam showcases the vibrant colors and tantalizing flavors of Vietnamese cuisine. It features mouth-watering images of signature dishes such as “bun bo hue” beautifully presented against a backdrop of Vietnamese cultural elements. The poster captures the essence of Co Mam as a place where food, culture, and community converge.

Xunder sweetsoup shop menu project .

The Xunder Sweet Soup Store menu design project captures the essence of Hong Kong's vibrant dessert culture with its visually appealing and culturally authentic menu. Inspired by bustling Hong Kong streets, the menu features a delightful selection of traditional sweet soups also known as “ tong sui”, alongside innovative creations like mochi peanut and sweet tofu pudding. Thoughtfully organized with bold typography and whimsical illustrations, each dish is accompanied by mouth-watering descriptions and funky illustration that showcase its vibrant colors and exquisite presentation. With its homage to cultural traditions and commitment to authenticity, the Xunder menu invites customers to indulge in a nostalgic culinary journey through Hong Kong's sweet delights.

college storytelling project .

This college project explores storytelling through a series of Illustrator illustrations, each capturing a different stage of a heartwarming narrative. Beginning with a simple black and white depiction of a cat lost in thought about having a home, the narrative progresses to a medium-level illustration showing the cat in a box being adopted, symbolizing the theme of companionship and belonging. Finally, the project culminates in a detailed illustration of the cat and it owner watching the sunset together, radiating warmth and contentment. Through varying levels of detail, these illustrations artfully convey the journey of finding home and happiness, showcasing the power of storytelling through visual imagery.
